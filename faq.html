Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We provide a range of transportation services, including long-distance student transport, airport transfers, corporate shuttles, and more. Visit our Services page for details.

How can I book a ride?

You can book your ride using our website or mobile app. Select your preferred service, input your travel details, and confirm your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment options, including credit/debit cards and mobile payments. Choose your preferred method at checkout.

What types of vehicles are available?

Our fleet includes sedans, SUVs, vans, and buses to suit different travel needs. You can select your preferred vehicle during booking.

Do you offer airport pickup and drop-off services?

Yes, we provide airport transfers. Choose the airport service when booking to ensure timely pickup or drop-off for your flight.

Are your vehicles accessible for people with disabilities?

We offer accessible vehicles. Please let us know your specific requirements when booking, and we'll provide the appropriate vehicle.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@transpo.com or call us at +123 456 7890. Our team is available 24/7.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking through your account on our website or app. Please check our Terms and Conditions for cancellation policies.

How do you ensure safety during rides?

We prioritize safety by conducting background checks on all drivers and maintaining our vehicles to the highest safety standards. Additionally, all rides are tracked in real-time.

Can I schedule a ride in advance?

Yes, you can schedule rides in advance through our website or mobile app. Simply select the date and time during the booking process.

Do you offer corporate transportation services?

Yes, we provide customized corporate transportation services for events, employee transport, and more.

What should I do if I left something in the vehicle?

If you left something behind, contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We will assist you in recovering your lost items.

Are pets allowed in the vehicles?

Yes, pets are allowed, but please inform us in advance so we can make necessary arrangements to ensure a comfortable trip for your pet.

What should I do if my driver is late?

If your driver is running late, track your ride through our app or contact customer support for updates.

Do you offer loyalty or reward programs?

Yes, we offer a loyalty program where you earn points for each ride. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future bookings.

About Us

We offer reliable and student-focused transport services. Our goal is to make your journey comfortable, safe, and affordable.
